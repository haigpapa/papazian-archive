# MAQAMAI
 
## Thesis
Before a model can be evaluated for maqam fidelity, the people evaluating it must learn to hear the relevant intervals and melodic behavior.
 
## Short Description
MaqamAI is a proposed browser-based ear-training tool for musicians, researchers, and engineers learning selected Arabic maqamat.
 
## Full Description
The learning sequence begins with curated references, interval recognition, melodic phrases, and guided comparison before introducing generated outputs. Early scope centers on a small set of maqamat rather than claiming comprehensive coverage. The project connects the Localization Gap’s audit findings to human listening practice and gives HAH-WAS reviewers a shared perceptual foundation. Its concept and tuning rules are documented; recordings, cultural review, lesson design, and a functional prototype remain required for public release.
 
## Highlights
- Cinematic Rail Type: 8-card secondary proof rail
- Initial scope: selected lessons in Rast, Bayati, and Hijaz
- Method: reference listening, interval recognition, phrase comparison, and model evaluation
- Status: product concept and tuning research documented; reviewed audio curriculum pending

5. COMPLETED / ARCHIVED PROJECTS

Space Time Tuning Machine (STTM)

Cross-disciplinary body of work following research on living, longing, and grieving in exile. An instrument not played by making sound from silence but in reverse — by tuning noises into sounds, melodies, and silence. Mimics how we recollect memories and dreams. The conceptual spine: a wandering troubadour who wakes up with no memory of songs, finds broken instruments, and with a migrating crane with broken wings builds a machine to guide their way home. Premiered at SXSW and The Broad Museum, Los Angeles. Conceptual lineage: Basinski → Caretaker → King Tubby → Eno → Pauline Oliveros.

Kardia — Somatic Synthesizer

Full PRD completed (January 2026)

React Native (Expo) / AudioKit (iOS) / Oboe (Android) / React Native Skia

Mobile biofeedback instrument transforming heart rate data into sound via the Iso-Principle (matching user's energy state with sonic texture, then guiding toward clarity as they calm). "Excavation, not addition." Start from dense harmonic chaos; calm the body to reveal the music.

#### Interaction:

3-zone vertical drag model; heart rate sets difficulty, hands shape character

#### Key design principle:

Don't display raw BPM (creates anxiety loops); abstract biosignal into texture

#### Architecture:

Offline-first (local SQLite), no account required for core use, Scala file export for cardiac scales

Animo (biosignal emotional communication), Iso-Principle from music therapy

6. INTELLECTUAL FRAMEWORKS & RESEARCH

The Localization Gap

#### Core thesis:

AI systems systematically erase cultural specificity — specifically, Arabic maqam systems, microtonal structures, and non-Western harmonic resolution — defaulting to Western frameworks.

#### Evidence base:

427+ tracks generated and annotated via Python API pipelines red-teaming Suno, Udio, and MusicLM

#### Application:

The intellectual spine of Haig's Anthropic application (Solutions Architect, Applied AI — Creatives role) and of Walaw Studio's AI research positioning

Not limited to music — AI's erasure of cultural specificity is a systemic design problem

Choreography of Systems

Core conceptual framework: treating interconnected disciplines as choreography rather than engineering. Authorship language (choreography, score, atlas) rather than engineering language (framework, pipeline, engine).

Diagonal Logic

Working across disciplines at angles — not horizontally (within a field) or vertically (depth in one field) but diagonally, finding the connective tissue.

The Meaning Stack

Architecture for understanding how meaning is constructed across layers — from raw material to cultural resonance.

Impossible Futures Framework

Framework for imagining and designing toward futures that are currently structurally blocked.

Navigating Latency

Framework for operating in conditions of systemic delay or friction.

Epistemic Defense

Framework for maintaining intellectual integrity in high-noise information environments.

Subtractive Composition

Music as excavation rather than addition. Influenced by: William Basinski (Disintegration Loops), The Caretaker, Pauline Oliveros (Deep Listening), King Tubby (mixing desk as subtractive instrument), Brian Eno (composition by subtraction), AMM, Berlin Reductionism.

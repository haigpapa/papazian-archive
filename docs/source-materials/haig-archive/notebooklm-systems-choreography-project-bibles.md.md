Core Curatorial Frame

The portfolio should not be organized primarily by medium, discipline, or industry category. Its strongest organizing logic is Systems Choreography: each project is a different mechanism for governing emergence, memory, cultural pressure, embodiment, or public infrastructure.

The main entry point into the method is Mashrou' Leila. The band is framed as the origin point: a 14-year counter-public cultural infrastructure that proved the stage can generate visibility but cannot protect the people gathered around it. The later work shifts from vulnerable performance architectures toward load-bearing systems, memory engines, sanctuaries, and protocols.

WHY WE'RE LIKE THIS is described as the most accessible explanatory gateway for non-specialist audiences. It can function as a public-intellectual bridge into the denser archive.

Twenty Project Slide Map

Use this as a compact reference for case-study cards, chapter openers, and project spine language.

Headline: Negentropic Generative Memory Engine

System hook: vector embeddings and ChromaDB navigate archival fragments through emotional adjacency rather than chronology.

Notes: DERIVE uses AI to preserve the fragmented condition of diasporic memory. The conductor/tuning layer prevents generative systems from smoothing rupture into fake narrative coherence.

Visual direction: dark latent space, floating archival fragments, Beirut photographs, Arabic/Armenian text, waveforms, precise digital vectors against decaying memory.

2. Space Time Tuning Machine

Headline: AI-Augmented Immersive Performance System

System hook: a hybrid instrument built from scrap electronics, hacked synths, violin resonance, ambient noise, and generative audio.

Notes: STTM reframes performance as conducting a human-machine system under pressure. Bricolage becomes the material culture of exile.

Visual direction: dim stage, wired violin, hacked electronics, Raspberry Pi circuitry, contact microphones, hard LED points in shadow.

3. STORYLINES

Headline: 3D Spatial Knowledge Graph

System hook: static literary archives become navigable 3D constellations through force-directed semantic mass.

Notes: STORYLINES proves that proximity can become a knowledge method. It rejects administrative order in favor of latent relationship.

Visual direction: deep-space graph, glowing literary nodes, luminous edges, dark void, thematic gravity.

4. Mashrou' Leila

Headline: Decade-Long Counter-Public Social System

System hook: a band operating as cultural infrastructure across censorship, surveillance, visibility, crisis, and collective identification.

Notes: Mashrou' Leila is the origin point of Systems Choreography. Its dissolution motivates the shift from vulnerable stages to durable infrastructure.

Visual direction: large nocturnal crowd, stage smoke, architectural lighting, defiant counter-public scale.

5. MEKENA NYC

Headline: Adaptive Reuse as Cultural Infrastructure

System hook: a 1920s Queens building becomes permanent live/work infrastructure for displaced artists.

Notes: MEKENA makes the method physical. Sustainability is framed as a prerequisite for cultural survival.

Visual direction: historic brick, warm live/work studios, sustainable systems, sensor readouts, grounded architectural precision.

6. Sometimes I Wake Up Elsewhere

Headline: Mobius-Twist Hypertext Fiction Engine

System hook: a looping autonomous narrative with temporal debt, document decay, bilingual reveals, and SparrowOS surveillance.

Notes: SIWUE turns exile into navigational mechanics. The reader inhabits displaced time rather than merely reading about it.

Visual direction: fractured text, glitch screens, avian surveillance, cold bureaucratic digital atmosphere.

Headline: AI-Human Generative Sonic Universe

System hook: ancestral archives and maqam patterns collide with generative models; glitches become future folklore.

Notes: TEBR treats AI failure as cultural evidence and compositional material.

Visual direction: diasporic futurism, ancient motifs, neon glitch, sonic landscape, majestic fragmentation.

Headline: Adversarial Veracity Protocol for Cultural AI

System hook: a reverse Turing test and epistemic defense system for detecting cultural hallucination and sonic erasure.

Notes: HAH-WAS shifts AI critique from opinion to protocol. It trains humans as forensic auditors of their own culture.

Visual direction: forensic UI, spectral graphs, Arabic waveform analysis, blue-white diagnostic light.

9. 3D-Beat-Synth

Headline: Somatic Input Spatial Audio UI

System hook: webcam-tracked hand motion becomes spatial sound synthesis through MediaPipe, Three.js, and Web Audio/Tone.js.

Notes: The body becomes a continuous input instrument, bypassing screen-bound clicking and proprietary VR hardware.

Visual direction: glowing hands sculpting geometric sound waves in a dark void.

10. Resonance Atlas

Headline: Interdisciplinary Research Initiative

System hook: maps the gap between human sonic experience and AI training-data limitations.

Notes: Resonance Atlas frames cultural erasure as both ethical failure and product-quality failure.

Visual direction: architectural data atlas, sonic blind spots, cool diagnostic light, research-map precision.

11. MaqamAI

Headline: Arabic Maqam Ear Training System

System hook: teaches engineers and musicians to hear Arabic microtones and the limits of the Western 12-TET grid.

Notes: You cannot engineer what you cannot hear. MaqamAI builds perceptual capacity as technical infrastructure.

Visual direction: microtonal waves, notation, precise educational interface, invisible intervals made visible.

12. The Autopsy / Beirut Phantom

Headline: Speculative Spatial Media

System hook: VR/browser photogrammetry reconstructs damaged Beirut spaces while preserving gaps and uncertainty.

Notes: incomplete data is not hidden; it becomes the narrative condition.

Visual direction: photorealism dissolving into wireframe, digital voids, temporal strata, architectural ghosts.

13. The Cartography of Absence

Headline: Bureaucratic Surrealism as Narrative System

System hook: exile is forced into forms, ledgers, audits, and administrative containers.

Notes: the project demonstrates bureaucratic violence from inside the form itself.

Visual direction: forms, passports, ledgers, red stamps, sterile fluorescent unease.

14. WHY WE'RE LIKE THIS

Headline: Poetic Documentary Series

System hook: video essays diagnose modern psychological and cultural conditions as systems with legible mechanics.

Notes: this is the public-facing bridge between dense systems thought and emotional legibility.

Visual direction: cinematic video essay stills, data visualizations, cognitive overload, sharp neon accents.

15. photon+

Headline: Optical Simulation Music Engine

System hook: music emerges from light interacting with geometric environments.

Notes: composition becomes environment design. The room writes the music.

Visual direction: WebGL optics, beams, mirrors, prisms, spectral color, scientific minimalism.

16. Codeverse Explorer

Headline: Visual Codebase Storyteller

System hook: static repositories become navigable 3D constellations through AST parsing and force-directed layouts.

Notes: a codebase is treated as a city, not a filing cabinet.

Headline: Somatic Biofeedback Synthesizer

System hook: HRV signals become generative soundscapes for autonomic regulation.

Notes: wellness data becomes acoustic feedback rather than anxious visual monitoring.

Visual direction: bioluminescent physiology, heart/nervous system signals, soft acoustic rings.

18. music-engines

Headline: Async Audio Analysis Kernel

System hook: backend infrastructure for audio ingestion, spectral analysis, transcription, feature extraction, and cultural audit data.

Notes: invisible research infrastructure powering DERIVE, HAH-WAS, TEBR, and Resonance Atlas.

Visual direction: industrial audio pipeline, server nodes, spectral streams, backend darkness.

19. Architecture in Low Res

Headline: The Epistemology of Digital Proxies

System hook: investigates how digital proxies like Google Earth alter perception of the built environment.

Notes: low resolution is not an error but a condition of mediated knowledge.

Visual direction: satellite view degrading into pixels, wireframes, architectural studio precision.

20. 1000 Strings at Rest

Headline: Multimedia Sound Sculpture Installation

System hook: silent tensioned string instruments embody music made unavailable by exile.

Notes: culture remains present under tension, waiting for a room that no longer exists.

Visual direction: solemn gallery, suspended strings, high-contrast spotlighting, latent acoustic potential.
